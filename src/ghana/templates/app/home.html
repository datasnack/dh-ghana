Welcome to the Ghana Hub

Give me a spin, I'm your Data Hub prototype in action!

The Data Hub serves as a dynamic spatio-temporal information platform, tailored to the data practices of researchers in infectious diseases and public stakeholders engaged in epidemic preparedness and response. It offers an integrated view of various Data Layers relevant to Ghana, spanning multiple categories such as socio-demographic, environmental, weather, health, and infrastructure. These layers are compiled from user-based and open data sources (with some layers originating from the same source) to support data analysis and decision-making.

Snack your Data

To access the gathered open data sources you can browse them via the Country, Region and District listings or the Study Site picker. Upon choosing your area of interest, you can tailor your data selection through our download configurator, which also offers a basic preview (spatial and temporal description) of the data for your convenience.
